Constitution of the following Statutory Authorities, IIT Jodhpur: Board of Governors Finance Committee Building & Works Committee Senate
Preparation of the Agenda and Minutes of the Statutory Bodies in coordination with respective Offices.
To ensure timely implementation of decisions of the Board of Governors (BoG) and other Statutory Authorities.
Ensuring compliance with institutional bylaws and regulatory guidelines.
To issue various Circulars/ Office Orders/ Office Memorandums/Notifications based on the Ministry of Education & other applicable Govt. of India Rules and associated with functioning of the Institute upon approval of the Competent Authority, from time to time.
Constitution of all Institute Level Committees.
Coordination with the Administrative Ministry, i.e., the Ministry of Education on various matters.
Management of Centralised Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System (CPGRAMS).
Facilitating training and professional development programs for Staff Members.
